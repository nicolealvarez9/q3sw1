WEATHERING WITH YOU




SYNOPSIS

A runaway high-school boy named Hodaka Morishima heads to Tokyo to find himself a fresh start for a new life. He finds that this new life of his is entangled with that of Hina Amano, a girl who he discovers can bring sunshine in a time of endless rain.

CHARACTERS
HINA AMANO
Hina is one of the two main characters in Weathering with You. Throughout the film, her unwavering and hardworking personality is displayed, along with her sunshine cheerfulness and abilities.
HODAKA MORISHIMA
Hodaka is the other main character in this film. He is a young student who rebelliously begins a new life in Tokyo, starting a job in a small and two-person-staffed editorial business. His life takes yet another turn when he meets Hina.
NAGI AMANO
Nagi Amano is Hina's younger brother. He is introduced as popular in his school, with girls in particular, but he is also gradually revealed to be a caring and appreciative young person.
KEISUKE SUGA
Keisuke is a troubled man who struggles with his own problems and habits of drinking and smoking, but is kind at heart. He helps Hodaka all throughout the film's story and unadmittedly cares deeply for the people around him.
NATSUMI SUGA
A playful and teasing but friendly and loyal woman is who Natsumi is. Just like her uncle Keisuke, she openly helps and cares for the good people in her life.
